Our features

Content Management

Your content needs to be fresh - You don't want to contact the developer every time you want to update a word or picture on your website. No coding required.

It is fast, elegant, and designed to give everyone on your team the tools they need to manage content at scale.

Members-only pages are only accessible to logged-in users.

Multilingual

Your audience speaks multiple languages - and now you do too, even if you personally don't.

Integrated translations help you replicate your content in every language you need.

Your visitor automatically sees your site in their own language, and they can easily switch it whenever they want.

Powerful Service

Requests are easily handled automatically with our configurable chat bot, directing users to the right solution.

Customized forms collect information in a standardized way, with optional GPS, and optional destinations.

Add on additional services and destinations through our integrations and API.

How does it work?

It's easy to get started

  • If you have an existing website, we can migrate all your legacy content, features, and structures
  • Enter your previous website or domain here: [text input]
  • Contact email address: [email input]
  • Within 1 business day we will contact you to deliver your new site

Mobile Friendly

  • Most of your users are on the go - so your website needs to be fast and easy to use on smart phones.
  • Responsive design ensures your site looks great on mobile devices as well as larger screens.
  • Fully responsive designs that look and work well on mobile devices (60% of traffic today)

Content

  • You can update your own content, text, photos, videos, and files, with the included Content Management System (CMS)
  • Your text content can be automatically translated into the languages you choose by the best machine translation (DeepL), and optionally reviewed by a professional translator (separate cost), or adjusted by you

Multilingual support

  • Automatic detection of your visitor’s browser language shows them your content in their language
  • The visitor can easily change the default language

Service

  • Requests for help and service are easily handled automatically with our configurable chat bot
  • Configurable contact forms, with optional GPS, and optional destinations

E-Commerce

  • Sell products with our optional E-Commerce section, which handles inventory, sales pages, pricing pages, payment, notifications, and reporting

Membership

  • Membership programs are supported by users sections, which are only accessible for logged-in users with a user account
  • Customer dashboard, for managing their account, orders, billing and invoices

Integrations

  • PagiTron - document management, data extraction, and workflows
  • Helpdesk - ticketing system with agent support
  • Basics - online database for your custom data needs
  • LiveStage - online streaming to an unlimited audience
  • Bangetti - ePaper for online publications and newsletters
  • BabelBoss - (included) integrated high-quality translations, done by machine, human, or both (separate cost).
